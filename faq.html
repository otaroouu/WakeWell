Frequently Asked Questions

Is it normal to feel aware while dreaming?

Some individuals experience increased awareness during certain sleep stages. This does not indicate risk and typically resolves without intervention.

Why do some dreams feel familiar?

The brain often reuses known environments and memories during sleep. Familiarity alone should not be considered unusual.

What if my dreams feel continuous?

Perceived continuity may occur when sleep cycles are disrupted. Maintaining a regular sleep schedule is strongly advised.

I feel like I stayed somewhere for a long time. Is that possible?

Time perception during sleep is subjective. Extended duration is not physically measurable and should not be interpreted literally.

What should I do if I feel disoriented after waking?

Mild disorientation can occur. If symptoms persist, avoid sleep journaling or memory recall exercises without professional guidance.

Why does WakeWell study this?

WakeWell is dedicated to public sleep safety and the prevention of abnormal sleep-state prolongation events.
